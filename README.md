# UniPay – Smart Campus Cashless Payment Ecosystem

## Overview

UniPay is a smart campus cashless payment ecosystem designed to modernize financial transactions within educational institutions. The platform enables students to perform secure and seamless payments using NFC-enabled identification cards, eliminating dependency on physical cash and reducing transaction time across campus facilities.

The system creates a unified digital payment infrastructure connecting students, merchants, and administrators through a centralized platform. UniPay provides real-time payment processing, wallet management, transaction monitoring, merchant operations, and administrative controls while ensuring security, transparency, and convenience.

By integrating NFC technology with a modern mobile application, UniPay delivers a fast, reliable, and scalable solution that enhances the overall campus experience and promotes digital transformation within educational environments.

---

## Frontend Design & User Experience

The frontend is developed using Flutter and follows a modern, clean, and responsive design approach. The application utilizes a role-based architecture to provide customized experiences for Students, Merchants, and Administrators while maintaining consistency across all interfaces.

The user interface focuses on simplicity, accessibility, and efficiency through intuitive navigation, visually appealing dashboards, real-time updates, and reusable UI components.

### Student Dashboard

The Student Dashboard serves as the primary hub for managing campus payments and NFC card activities.

Students can:

- View wallet balance in real time
- Monitor NFC card status
- Access transaction history
- Track recent payment activity
- Add money to their wallet
- Manage card settings and controls
- Review spending patterns through transaction records

The dashboard is designed to provide complete visibility over financial activities while ensuring quick access to frequently used features.

### Merchant Dashboard

The Merchant Dashboard enables campus vendors and service providers to efficiently manage payments and earnings.

Merchants can:

- Monitor total earnings
- View incoming NFC transactions
- Access detailed payment records
- Track recent customer activity
- Verify successful transactions
- Manage daily financial operations

The interface presents critical business information in a structured and easy-to-understand format, improving operational efficiency.

### Admin Console

The Admin Console acts as the central management system for the entire platform.

Administrators can:

- Register and manage merchants
- Manage student accounts
- Monitor platform activity
- Oversee NFC card operations
- Control user access and permissions
- Maintain system-wide operations

The dashboard is designed to simplify administrative workflows while ensuring effective monitoring and control.

This ensures complete transparency and simplifies auditing, reporting, and transaction verification.

## System Workflow

1. Students receive an NFC-enabled identification card linked to their UniPay wallet.
2. Funds are added to the wallet through authorized channels.
3. Students make payments by tapping their NFC card at merchant terminals.
4. The system validates the card and processes the transaction.
5. Wallet balances and merchant earnings are updated instantly.
6. Transaction records are stored and displayed in real time.
7. Administrators monitor and manage platform operations through the Admin Console.
